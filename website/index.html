Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we use end-to-end encryption and comply with HIPAA regulations. Your conversations and personal data are completely secure and confidential.

How does the AI therapist work?

Our AI therapist uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning to provide personalized responses based on evidence-based therapeutic techniques.

Can I switch between AI and human therapists?

Absolutely! You can use our AI therapist for immediate support and book sessions with human therapists for more complex issues or ongoing therapy.

What if I need immediate crisis support?

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services immediately. Our platform is designed for ongoing support, not emergency situations.
